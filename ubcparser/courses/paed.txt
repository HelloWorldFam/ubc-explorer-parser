

code: PAED 430
name: Paediatrics
cred: 8
desc: In-patient experience; experience in ambulatory clinics. Clinical teaching in small groups and Academic Half-Days, which supply a series of lectures and seminars. Emphasis on acquisition of clinical skills and a knowledge base of general paediatrics. Clinical duties include patient histories, physical examinations, participation in investigation and management, following patient progress, taking part in daily rounds, and night call.
