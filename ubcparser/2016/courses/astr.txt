

code: ASTR 101
name: Introduction to the Solar System

code: ASTR 102
name: Introduction to Stars and Galaxies
cred: 3
desc: Modern stellar and extragalactic astronomy. Stars and stellar evolution from protostars to black holes; galaxies and quasars; cosmology. ASTR 200/205 rather than ASTR 102 are recommended for students who intend to pursue studies in Astronomy. [3-2*-0]
preq: PHYS 11 or PHYS 12 or PHYS 100. plus Principles of Mathematics 12

code: ASTR 200
name: Frontiers of Astrophysics
cred: 3
desc: Stellar masses and evolution. White dwarfs, neutron stars, and black holes. Extrasolar planet formation and detection. Dark matter in the Milky Way and other galaxies. Cosmological observations and principles. Weather permitting, observations will be conducted. [3-0-0]
preq: (PHYS 101 or PHYS 107 or PHYS 117 or PHYS 157 or PHYS 153) and ((MATH 101 or MATH 103 or MATH 105 or MATH 121;) or SCIE 001)

code: ASTR 205
name: Stars and Stellar Populations
cred: 3
desc: Measurement of positions, motions, and distances in astronomy.  Temperature, masses, and spectra of stars.  Hertzsprung-Russell diagram.  Quantitative stellar structure and evolution.  Chemical composition of stars in different Galactic populations. Globular and open star clusters. [3-0-0]
preq: ASTR 200 and (PHYS 210 or EOSC 211 or CPSC 302 or CPSC 303)

code: ASTR 300
name: Galaxies
cred: 3
desc: Structure and kinematics of our galaxy. Spiral arms and dynamics of stars in spiral and elliptical galaxies. Galactic formation, evolution, dynamics, and groups. Active galaxies and quasars. 
preq: (ASTR 202 or ASTR 205) and (PHYS 210 or EOSC 211 or CPSC 302 or CPSC 303)
creq: MATH 217 or MATH 227 or MATH 317

code: ASTR 310
name: Exploring the Universe: The Solar System
cred: 3
desc: A survey of recent discoveries about the planets and other objects in the solar system, without the use of advanced mathematics. The Sun, the existence of planetary systems around other stars, and the search for life. Not open to first year students and not for credit in the Faculties of Science and Applied Science. [3-0-1*]

code: ASTR 311
name: Exploring the Universe: Stars and Galaxies
cred: 3
desc: A survey of recent discoveries in modern astronomy without the use of advanced mathematics. Stars, pulsars, black holes, galaxies, quasars and the origin and evolution of the Universe. Not open to first year students and not for credit in the Faculties of Science and Applied Science. [3-0-1*]
