

code: VISA 110

code: VISA 183

code: VISA 241
name: Introduction to Digital Photography
cred: 3
desc: Introduction to digital photography image creation in relation to contemporary art. Emphasis on digital camera use, file management, and digital print production.  Only one of VISA 240 and VISA 241 may be used toward the program requirements of the B.F.A. Visual Art and the B.A. Visual Art programs.
